PoseCap — Auto-capture your moments.

An iPhone app for creators, parents, and pet lovers. PoseCap detects people, faces, and animals on-device and captures the moment for you — single lens, dual lens, or slow motion.

Requires iPhone or iPad with iOS 15.6 or later.

Main features

The ultimate hands-free capture tool. Auto Capture is designed to make content creation smoother and is kid-friendly. It also captures animal moments — perfect for pet lovers.

Automatic Video Recording

Captures moments automatically when humans, faces, or animals are detected.

Single Lens

Captures moments with a single lens for focused, simple shots.

Dual Lens

Records two videos simultaneously across two lenses.

Slow Motion

Captures moments in slow motion for cinematic replays.
