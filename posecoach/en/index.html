PoseCoach — your AI badminton motion coach.

Capture your full-body swing in 3D with ARKit, get instant scoring on speed, stability, tempo and technique, and chat with an AI coach about every motion — all on your iPhone, no account required.

Join the TestFlight beta See what's inside

Requires iPhone with A12 Bionic or later, iOS 13+. Apple Watch optional (watchOS 7+).

Built around your swing

Six pillars that turn your iPhone into a coach you can carry to every session.

3D motion capture

ARKit tracks 91 joints in 3D world coordinates with quaternion rotation — full-body, full-motion, no markers.

AI coach chat

Multi-turn conversation about your motion — ask for feedback, drills, or comparisons. Available in English and Chinese.

Automatic swing detection

PoseCoach finds each swing automatically and breaks it into peak energy, hand velocity, and swing-phase segments.

Four-dimension scoring

Each shot gets a 0–100 score across speed, stability, tempo and technique — easy to track session over session.

Slow-motion replay with AI timeline

Scrub through the swing in slow motion with the AI's commentary anchored to each phase of the motion.

Apple Watch integration

Optional heart-rate fusion and exercise-ring credit during capture. Use your Watch to monitor remotely.

Private by design

Pose detection, scoring, and playback all run on your device. The AI coach analyzes anonymous skeleton data on our servers — your raw video and images never leave your iPhone.

  • On-device pose recognition and scoring (Apple Foundation Models on supported hardware)
  • AI coach uploads only anonymized skeleton data; raw video and images are never uploaded
  • No account required
  • Export sessions as binary (.bin) or CSV via AirDrop, Photos, Files or ZIP

Track your progress

Every session is saved on-device. Browse a long-term motion history and watch your scores improve.
