Pose Coach is your AI sports motion coach.

Record your training with your iPhone and let AI break down your movement, then tell you exactly what to fix next. Badminton and running today, with auto-cut highlight reels and a coach you can chat with.

What's inside

An AI coach that explains what to fix

Ask in plain language and get multi-turn feedback on your recording. Tap any timestamp to jump straight to the moment the coach is talking about. On-device AI on iOS 26+.

Compare your form to a reference you choose

Place your clip side by side with a reference you pick — your best rep, a saved session, or one shared with you. Watch the two play together and spot the difference.

Clear scores, clear next steps

Every rep is graded S/A/B/C/D across four dimensions, with the top 3 actionable tips so you always know what to work on next.
