About PoseCap Inc.

PoseCap Inc. is a small team building motion-intelligence software. We started with PoseCap, an iPhone app that auto-captures the moment a pose snaps into place. We're now extending the same on-device motion stack to athletes (Pose Coach) and to the people who study them (Pose CLI).

All three products share one motion data format and one analysis core, so a recording made on your iPhone can flow directly into a research-grade report on your laptop without re-capture.

What we believe

  • Local first. Pose detection, scoring and AI coaching run on the device whenever possible.
  • One data format. Our 13-column JointData3D representation works the same on iPhone, Watch and CLI.
  • Useful, not magical. We'd rather ship a single reproducible metric than a black-box "score".
